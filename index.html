    CS 200 Final Project

    For my final project I created an adventure game based off the recent Jumanji movies. In the game the player is tasked with finding a jewel and uses the keyboard to move around the map. They can also check their surroundings which will play sounds and provide text clues. I used a variety of loops (for, do-while, etc) and coding techniques I learned from the course such as if-else statements and arrays.
