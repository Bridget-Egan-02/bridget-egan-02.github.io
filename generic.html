About Me

My name is Bridget Egan. I am a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Computer Science.

I am in serveral clubs such as UW-Madison's Girls Who Code, Coding for Good and a sorority, Delta Delta Delta. In my free time I enjoy golfing, hanging out with friends and coding. I was introduced to computer science in high school when I took my first computer science class and fell in love. I was raised in Breckenridge, CO and later moved to Lake Geneva, WI. Also, I was born deaf and have a cochlear implant and a hearing aid. I currently work at Bath and Body Works over the summer. In the past I have worked at The Candle Mercantile, The Geneva Theatre, Geneva National and Grand Geneva as a ski instructor.
